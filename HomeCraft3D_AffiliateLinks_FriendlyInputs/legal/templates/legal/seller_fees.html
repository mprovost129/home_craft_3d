Seller Fees

Home Craft 3D charges a 15% fee on each completed sale. This fee covers payment processing, platform maintenance, and support.

How the Fee Works

  • The 15% fee is automatically deducted from the sale price of each order.
  • The remaining amount is paid out to you (the seller).
  • There are no additional hidden charges.

Example Calculations

Example 1:
Product price: $20.00
Seller fee (15%): $3.00
Amount you receive: $17.00
Example 2:
Product price: $100.00
Seller fee (15%): $15.00
Amount you receive: $85.00

The fee is automatically deducted from your payout. If you have questions, please contact support.
