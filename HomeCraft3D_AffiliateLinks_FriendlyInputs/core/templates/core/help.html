Help

Quick links and guidance for using Home Craft 3D.
Buying
  • Browse products and add items to your cart.
  • Digital downloads appear in My Orders → Purchases & downloads after payment.
  • Refunds are available for physical items only, at seller discretion.
Selling
  • Create listings as drafts first; you can edit drafts freely.
  • To activate paid or physical listings you must complete Stripe onboarding.
  • You may activate a limited number of free digital file listings before onboarding.
Email verification
Some actions require a verified email: posting Q&A, starting seller onboarding, and leaving reviews. If you’re blocked, visit your Profile to resend verification.
