About Home Craft 3D

A marketplace for 3D printed models and downloadable 3D files.

Home Craft 3D connects makers and collectors. Sellers can list physical prints (printed and shipped) and digital files (STL/3MF/OBJ) for download.

Physical prints
Printed and shipped by the seller. Each listing includes a flat shipping cost set by the seller.
Digital files
Downloadable after payment. Re-downloads are always available from your Purchases page.
